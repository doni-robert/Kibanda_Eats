Intro Feature About

Kibanda Eats - Discover local eateries for delicious and affordable foods



Sign Up

The app has the ability to accept new users through the sign up page. All you need is an email, username and password. After that, you can login and add a post



Add Post

A user can add a post through simple steps. Input the description of the meal, its price, location where the food can be found, a comment on the food and finally upload of the photo



Gallery

Even without logging in, anyon can use the app by viewing its gallery of images. A search feature is availabe where one can search by either location or price
